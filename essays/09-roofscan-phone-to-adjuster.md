# RoofScan AI: Turning a Phone Camera Into an Insurance Adjuster

A roofer takes a photo. AI detects hail damage, wind damage, missing shingles, age deterioration. Auto-generates a PDF inspection report with confidence scores. Client-ready. Branded.

## The market

Insurance adjusting is a $12B market in the US alone. Every adjuster does the same three things:

1. Climb the roof
2. Take photos
3. Write the report

RoofScan AI does steps 2 and 3 in seconds.
